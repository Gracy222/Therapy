  • What services does NaijaHealth offer?
    NaijaHealth offers various somatic therapy services. They incluse: Massage therapy, Chiropractic therapy, Physiotherapy, Cold and Heat therapy, and so on.
  • Are the therapist at NaijaHealth qualified?.
    Yes, every therapy practitioners at NaijaHealth are qualified..
  • What makes NaijaHealth stand out from other healthcare providers?
    NaijaHealth stands out for its personalized approach to healthcare, integrating both modern and traditional therapies to ensure comprehensive care for every individual.
  • How can I book an appointment with NaijaHealth?
    You can easily book an appointment online through our website or by calling our customer service hotline to schedule a session with any of our expert therapists.
  • What are the benefits of Physiotherapy at NaijaHealth?
    Physiotherapy at NaijaHealth helps in rehabilitation, improving mobility, reducing pain, and enhancing overall body functionality through tailored exercises and treatments.
  • What is Chiropractic therapy?
    Chiropractic therapy at NaijaHealth involves hands-on spinal manipulation and other manual techniques to treat musculoskeletal pain, especially related to the spine, joints, and muscles.
  • Is massage therapy available at NaijaHealth?
    Yes, massage therapy is available and is used to reduce stress, relieve pain, and enhance overall well-being by improving circulation and relaxing the muscles
  • Can I use NaijaHealth's services for both preventive and therapeutic care?
    Absolutely! NaijaHealth offers both preventive and therapeutic care. Our therapies are designed to help maintain wellness and treat existing health concerns.
