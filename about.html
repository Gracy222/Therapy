About Us

At NaijaHealth, we are dedicated to fostering holistic healing for Nigerians through somatic therapy.
Our clinic provides a safe, culturally sensitive space where mind-body connection and emotional healing take center stage. We focus on trauma-informed care, stress management, and personalized therapy to support each individual’s wellness journey. With a team of highly skilled professionals, we aim to help you achieve physical, emotional, and mental balance.

Our History

NaijaHealth was founded with a vision to bring accessible, high-quality somatic therapy to Nigeria. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted resource for holistic healing, combining traditional therapeutic practices with modern techniques to provide effective care. Our journey has been fueled by our commitment to enhancing the well-being of our community and empowering individuals to live healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Why Choose Us

Choose NaijaHealth for expert somatic therapy tailored to your unique needs.
We combine a holistic approach with culturally relevant care, experienced professionals, and personalized therapy plans to support your journey to optimal health. Trust us to help you achieve balance and well-being.

Somatic therapy is the practice of using body awareness techniques to address a variety of physical and emotional challenges, including stress, trauma, and chronic pain. Through somatic movements and body-focused interventions, we work to restore balance and healing, promoting overall wellness.

At NaijaHealth, our therapy team is ready to work with you to understand your unique needs. Together, we’ll create a personalized plan that supports your mind-body connection. We look forward to helping you on your journey to wellness.
